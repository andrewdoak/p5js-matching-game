Match the Number

Are you fast, or just furious?

A game = ten attempts.

Six matches or more = A WIN.

Three misses = A LOSS!

1. Type the number you see when it comes at you.

2. If your key matches the number, you score. The screen will flash a (random) color.

3. If you miss (type...pun intended), the screen will flash red. You don't HAVE to try every round.

4. Game over? Press enter to play again. And play on a computer, please!
